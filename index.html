About me

Hi! I'm Rafael. I'm a Researcher at Técnico Lisboa   (where I also received my BSc, MSc and most recently, my PhD in Information Systems and Computer Science). There, I work with the Visualization and Intelligent Multimodal Interfaces Group at INESC-ID Lisbon. However, my main research position is working in the Blackbox Project at FCSH , where the result of my PhD research (and more) is being applied to dance and performing arts scenarios. Current interest topics are video-based rendering, point cloud processing/rendering, and recently also VR and AR.
